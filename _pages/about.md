I am an undergraduate student specializing in computer science with a keen interest in data science and machine learning. My goal is to leverage my knowledge to develop efficient and impactful projects.

I have proficiency in __Python and C++__, and experience with relational database systems such as PostgreSQL and MySQL, primarily for backend support in my projects.

I am actively working on several projects hosted on GitHub, including:
- A Django web application demonstrating stable matchings
- A Flask web application demonstrating NYC vehicle collusions with prediction analysis and more.
- A document parsing script that splits documents into smaller chunks (completely user-modifiable).
- Q-learning based RL implementation of a research paper

I'm currently working as a data science intern for MetLife, where I work alongside a talented team of data scientists and ML engineers. This internship provided me a lot of insight into financial use-cases for data science & ML. 

In addition to my major, I have minored in Asian-American studies, where I explored historical literature from distinguished Asian-American authors such as Arundhati Roy, Carlos Bulosan, Maxine Hong Kingston, John Okada, Hisaye Yamamoto, and others. These works have significantly influenced my personal and academic growth.

Special thanks to my friends, mentors & professors; their teachings on not just academia, but life, have been invaluable.
