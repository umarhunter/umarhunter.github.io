I'm an undergrad studying the science of computers. Specifically focusing on data science and machine learning. My goal is to apply my knowledge to develop efficient and impactful projects.

I have proficiency in __Python and C++__, and experience with relational database systems such as PostgreSQL and MySQL, although primarily for database integration in my personal projects.

I'm currently working as a data science intern for MetLife, where I work alongside a talented team of data scientists and ML engineers who help me learn more practical skills. This internship provided me a lot of insight into financial use-cases for data science & ML. 

In addition to my major, I have minored in Asian-American studies, where I explored historical literature from distinguished Asian-American authors such as Arundhati Roy, Carlos Bulosan, Maxine Hong Kingston, John Okada, Hisaye Yamamoto, and others. These works have significantly influenced my personal and academic growth. I'm also a recipient of the [2023-2024 Asian-American Scholarship for AAS Minors](https://www.hunter.cuny.edu/aasp).

Special thanks to my friends, mentors & professors; their teachings on not just academia, but life, have been invaluable.
