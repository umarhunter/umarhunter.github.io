Hey, I'm an undergrad studying the science of computers. Specifically focusing on data science and machine learning. My goal is to apply my knowledge to develop efficient and impactful projects.

I have proficiency in __Python and C++__, and experience with relational database systems such as PostgreSQL and MySQL, primarily for backend support in my projects.

I am actively working on several projects hosted on GitHub, including:
- A Django web application demonstrating stable matchings
- A Flask web application demonstrating NYC vehicle collusions with prediction analysis and more.
- A document parsing script that splits documents into smaller chunks (completely user-modifiable).
- Q-learning based RL implementation of a research paper

I'm currently working as a data science intern for MetLife, where I work alongside a talented team of data scientists and ML engineers and learn more practical skills. This internship provided me a lot of insight into financial use-cases for data science & ML. 

In addition to my major, I have minored in Asian-American studies, where I explored historical literature from distinguished Asian-American authors such as Arundhati Roy, Carlos Bulosan, Maxine Hong Kingston, John Okada, Hisaye Yamamoto, and others. These works have significantly influenced my personal and academic growth. I'm also a recipient of the [https://www.hunter.cuny.edu/aasp](2023-2024 Asian-American Scholarship for AAS Minors).

Special thanks to my friends, mentors & professors; their teachings on not just academia, but life, have been invaluable.
